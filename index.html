📖 How to Use
1

Open a Sales Invoice in Manager.io

Click your EFRIS custom button — invoice details load automatically.

2

Review the details shown

Check customer name, amount and items.

3

Click "Submit to URA EFRIS"

Allow 10–20 seconds for URA to respond.

4

FDN saved automatically

Written to the EFRIS FDN field on your Manager invoice.

5

Use Goods tab to configure items

Browse URA commodity tree and sync items to Manager.io as inventory or non-inventory items.

Sad face icon in Manager panel?
Manager requires HTTPS. Your custom button URL must be HTTPS — use your Cloudflare Tunnel URL:
https://goods.twoservants.com/extension
Authentication failed?
Check TIN, Device No. and EFRIS Password in Settings.
Manager not saving?
Check access token and that custom fields exist in Manager Settings → Custom Fields → Sales Invoices.
Goods not appearing in URA?
Enable “Also register in EFRIS goods registry” toggle in the Goods tab when syncing items.
